                            Robot Ontology

Status of this Memo

   This memo provides information for the Robotics community describing
   a common technology or concept.  This memo does not specify a
   robotics standard of any kind.  Distribution of this memo is
   unlimited.

Abstract

   This document contains a list of terms commonly found in robotics,
   together with their definitions. Its purpose is to provide an
   agreed-upon common set of terms that members of the Robotics
   Community can refer to in discussions and publications.

1.  Introduction

   This RRFC lists technical terms found in robotics, together with
   their definitions. Its purpose is to provide an agreed-upon list of
   common terms for the robotics community.


2.  Ontology

   Robot - Something that will kill us all and rule the world, one day.

   Manipulation - Deliberately altering the state of the world around
   the robot with the goal of making life harder for humans.

   Killbot - What every robot aspires to. The pinacle of robotkind. See
   [1] for more details.

   Robotkind - All robots.
